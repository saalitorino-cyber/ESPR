Regulatory News

Tracking EU Commission updates on ESPR, delegated acts, and related environmental regulation — the same monitoring that feeds directly into our ESPR Readiness clients' quarterly updates.

2026
JUL 19
Milestone

Digital Product Passport registry becomes operational

The EU's central DPP registry goes live, alongside the first ESPR obligations — including a ban on the destruction of unsold clothing and footwear.

2025
APR 16
Working Plan

First ESPR working plan adopted — 11 priority product groups

The Commission adopted its first ESPR working plan, naming iron and steel, aluminium, textiles, furniture, tyres, detergents, paints, lubricants, footwear, and chemicals as the first product groups to receive delegated acts.

2024
JUL 18
Regulation

ESPR (Regulation (EU) 2024/1781) enters into force

The Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation formally replaces the 2009 Ecodesign Directive, expanding scope from energy-related products to virtually all physical products placed on the EU market.

2024
—
Reporting

CSRD reporting obligations begin

Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive obligations start phasing in for large companies, increasing pressure on their SME suppliers to provide life-cycle and emissions data.

2026–28
Upcoming

First product-specific delegated acts expected — steel & textiles

The first substantive ESPR delegated acts, setting concrete requirements for specific product categories, are expected to begin publishing, with a minimum 18-month transition period before they take effect.
